    VISION

    To empower and inspire the youth in our community by providing them with opportunities to excel in the fields of entertainment, media, and entrepreneurship, ultimately fostering a generation of skilled and innovative leaders who contribute positively to society.

    MISSION

    to empowering the youth in our community through the provision of job opportunities, training, and resources in the entertainment and media industries. By promoting talent development, fostering creativity, and driving positive social impact, we aim to cultivate a generation of skilled and visionary leaders who shape the future of our society.

    GAOLS

    1. Create sustainable job opportunities: Establish a platform that enables youth to gain employment and entrepreneurial skills in the entertainment and media industries, reducing unemployment rates and promoting economic growth in our community. 2. Promote talent development: Identify, nurture, and showcase the diverse talents of young individuals in acting, filmmaking, music production, and media through training programs, workshops, and mentorship initiatives.

    GAOLS

    3. Foster creativity and innovation: Cultivate an environment that encourages creativity, innovation, and collaboration among youth, empowering them to express themselves artistically and contribute fresh perspectives to the entertainment and media sectors. 4. Promote social inclusion and diversity: Ensure that African Star Vision is inclusive and accessible to all youth, regardless of their background, gender, or socio-economic status, and celebrate the rich diversity of talent within our community. 5. Drive positive social impact: Use the power of entertainment and media to address social issues, raise awareness about important causes, and inspire positive change, contributing to the overall well-being and development of our society.


    THIS IS OUR STORY

    In 2020, amidst the challenging conditions of Kakuma Refugee Camp, a group of determined young individuals came together with a shared dream of harnessing the power of entertainment and media to create opportunities for themselves and their peers.
